AeroSense is an AI-powered air quality prediction platform developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge. 
It leverages NASA Earth observation data and OpenAQ datasets to predict air quality levels and analyze their impact on human health.

The system provides real-time AQI predictions, multi-pollutant analysis (PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, CO, Ozone), and health risk insights based on WHO guidelines. 
It also integrates NASA TEMPO satellite data for high-resolution environmental monitoring.

From EarthData to Action, AeroSense transforms environmental data into meaningful insights for better decision-making and public health awareness.
